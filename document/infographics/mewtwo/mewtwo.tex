\vspace{-0.5cm}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \ghostfull~Giratina is better than Mewtwo. However, Giratina has two weaknesses \icefull~\fairyfull~than Mewtwo doesn't have.
  \item \psychicfull~Mewtwo is the best \psychicfull~Pok√©mon, although Psychic has no utility until gen 5.
  \item \fightingfull~Machamp is better than Mewtwo. However, Mewtwo is not weak to \psychicfull, making it usefull versus bosses having \psychicfull~moves (Arceus, Regigigas).
  \item \electricfull~Raikou and Magnezone are better than Mewtwo.
  \item \normalfull~Hyper Beam is not optimal.
  \item \icefull~Mamoswine, Glaceon and Weawile are better than Mewtwo. However, Mewtwo doesn't have \firefull~\grassfull~weakness, which makes it usefull versus Groudon.
  \item \firefull~Moltres and Entei are better than Mewtwo.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Against other Pok\'emon}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \fightingfull~Mewtwo can counter Blissey, Snorlax, Chansey, Rhyperior, Tyranitar and all \normalfull~\rockfull~Pok\'emon.
  \item \ghostfull~Mewtwo can counter Espeon, Gardevoir, Metagross.
  \item \icefull~Mewtwo can counter Dragonite, Salamence, Togekiss, Groudon.
\end{itemize}
